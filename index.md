### Researcher:
#### Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Vision, Data Analysis, and Sustainable ICT
#### I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Eastern Finland with a background that combines academic research and teaching with more than 20 years of industrial experience in automation, software, imaging systems, and technical development.
#### My work focuses on applied AI, machine vision, OCR/text recognition, data-driven methods, and sustainability-oriented ICT development.
#### My doctoral dissertation addressed the recognition of batch and expiration date codes on curved pharmaceutical packaging and developed deep learning-based methods for solving challenging industrial text recognition problems.
#### _"Jarmo Koponen has extensive work experience in the industry, which has clearly been reflected as an above-average level of expertise and the ability to conceptualize and construct large-scale entities."_-Prof. P. Toivanen, the supervisor of my dissertation work, stated on 10.02.2021.
